Pizza Formagi

Description

This pizza recipe is extremely delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Yellow cheese
  • Mozarella

Steps to prepare:

  1. Make some homemade dough
  2. Stretch it in one big tray
  3. Pour some tomato sauce and smear it equally
  4. Put all the cheeses on top
