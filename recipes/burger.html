Cheeseburger

Description

This burger recipe is extremely delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Burger bread
  • Patty
  • American cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce

Steps to prepare:

  1. Bake the bread
  2. Roast the patty
  3. Slice some Tomatoes
  4. Put the american cheese on top of the patty
  5. Assemble the burger
