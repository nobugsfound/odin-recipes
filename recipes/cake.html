Cake

Description

This cake was favourite as a child, but now I know how bad it is

Ingredients:

  • Swamp
  • Vanilla cream
  • Sugar
  • Sprinkles
  • Syrup

Steps to prepare:

  1. Bake the Swamp
  2. Put on the cream with Sugar
  3. Put on the chocolate sprinkles
  4. Leave in the fridge for 1 hour
  5. Cut the cake or eat it like a savage as it's full
